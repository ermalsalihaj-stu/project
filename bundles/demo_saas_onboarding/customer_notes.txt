Customer notes / insights (mixed sources)

- Enterprise prospects say SSO is table-stakes. Without SAML/SSO they cannot pass security review.
- New users report confusion during onboarding: too many fields, unclear next steps, and weak guidance.
- Users are asking for a guided checklist or “setup assistant” to reduce time-to-value.
- Some users abandon right after signup because they don’t know what to do first.
- Support reports more tickets about login/reset and MFA confusion.
- Teams with accessibility requirements flagged that some onboarding steps are hard with screen readers.
