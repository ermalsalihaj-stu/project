# Product Request: Checkout Flow Improvements

We need to improve the checkout and billing experience for our SaaS product. Enterprise customers are churning due to unclear pricing and complex billing workflows. SMB users report confusion around subscription tiers and payment methods.

**Scope:** Focus on the checkout funnel (cart → payment → confirmation) and billing dashboard. We want to reduce drop-off, clarify pricing, and ensure we meet enterprise requirements (including SSO and GDPR-compliant data handling for billing records).
