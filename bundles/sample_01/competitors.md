# Competitors Analysis

## Competitor Alpha

**What they do well:** One-page checkout with inline validation. Clear pricing breakdown before payment. Strong enterprise SSO integration.

**What they do badly:** Invoice export is PDF-only, no API. Limited payment methods for SMB.

---

## Competitor Beta

**What they do well:** Stripe-native billing dashboard. Excellent documentation for developers. GDPR-ready by default with EU data residency toggle.

**What they do badly:** No guest checkout. Enterprise procurement is slow (manual quote process).

---

## Competitor Gamma

**What they do well:** Accessibility-first checkout (WCAG 2.1 AA certified). Prominent progress indicator. Saved payment methods with one-click reorder.

**What they do badly:** Opaque pricing for add-ons. High support ticket volume for billing questions.

---

## Competitor Delta

**What they do well:** Self-serve upgrade with instant activation. Clean invoice history with line-item detail.

**What they do badly:** Login required for everything; no guest checkout. Weak screen reader support in checkout form.

---

## Competitor Epsilon

**What they do well:** Flexible billing cycles. Granular invoice export (CSV, PDF, API). Strong SOC2 positioning.

**What they do badly:** Checkout flow is 7 steps. Confusing tier comparison.
